Why this blotchy thing as the logo?

Well, it could be from an artist's ink, symbolizing creativity. It is also an unformed shape. Like a stem cell, it can turn into a lot of more concrete things down the road (This portfolio too, btw). An ink blob is also somewhat rebellious. It doesn't care about the author's original intentions. It just sits there, looking at you.

In other words, the blob seemed to be on brand, at least for now.
